The Event Of The Year



Aman Dhattarwal at Gyan Ganga Group of Institutions Jabalpur

The event “Shaping Your B.Tech & Spandan”, which comprised of two educational & motivational sessions for two different student groups was hosted by the Department of Computer Science and Information Technology and was organized by Team Indigo GGITS. The guest speaker for the event was Mr. Aman Dhattarwal who is a popular personality among the Indian youth. Dated 22nd February 2020, the 1 st seminar for high school students was attended by more than 100 students and the 2 nd seminar for college students was attended by more than 500 undergrads. Also, we observed the gracious presence of Honorable Directors of the Gyan Ganga Group, Respected Principals of GGITS and GGCT and Respected Head of Departments. Completely organized and managed by the students of the first year, the event was a grand success and the seminars proved to be very beneficial and helpful for the students.



Blogs Section

Indigo Triwizard Contest

This competition was organized under two subcategories - Story Writing and Article Writing. The contest was created using Google Forms. More than 100 students participated from GGITS and GGCT 1st year all branches. After given a submission period of one week, top 10 winners in both categories were announced. The achievement certificates have been provided to the winners and their prizes will be distributed in our physical live events in the future. This was our first event under a non-technical theme and artistic subject.

Unique and Insightful Motivational Webinar by Er. Pankaj Rai

This was a unique informational, motivational and self-help webinar by guest speaker Mr. Pankaj Rai. Also, He has previously been a guest speaker at Gyan Ganga in Freshers Inductiom Program 2019. The session was attended by 100+ students and was a very energizing and refreshing one. A lot of queries from students were answered by the guest speaker as he a psychologist and NLP practitioner. The online event was covered by various Instagram pages.

Pie & AI Event by Deeplearning.AI – “Breaking into AI”

This online live event was affiliated and acclaimed by Deeplearning.AI as we applied for the student ambassadorship of the same. The theme of the session was - "Breaking into AI" and the speaker was Mr. Muditya Raghav. The event was joined by 100+ students (including 20- 25 from other colleges) on Google Meets platform. We are now Deeplearning.AI ambassadors for Jabalpur city.
