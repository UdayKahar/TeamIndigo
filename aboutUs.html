ABOUT US

Team Indigo is a students' society under the Gyan Ganga Group and as of now - FOX Academy Campus Ambassador, Deeplearning.AI Global Ambassador for Jabalpur City, and Campus Ninja (Coding Ninja) - are formed and run by us in GGITS and GGCT. Various technical, non-technical, and self-help events and activities are conducted by the society. We conduct periodic workshops and seminars based on trending technologies. Several major and minor events (including online events) have been conducted by the society under my leadership and management. Club teams also participate in Hackathons and technical competitions. Also, live sessions with popular social media personalities, experienced industry experts and successfully placed seniors, updates on the latest online learning resources, information about the latest technical competitions & internships, etc. are our regular club activities.
